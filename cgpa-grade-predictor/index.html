Smart Tips
💡

Weight High-Credit Subjects

A 4-credit subject impacts your CGPA twice as much as a 2-credit one. Prioritize them.

📊

Check Early, Not Late

Run this calculation after every exam. The more subjects remain, the easier to course-correct.

🎯

Set a Stretch Target

Aim 0.2–0.3 above your actual goal. Buffer for one bad day without losing your CGPA.

🔁

Recalculate Every Sem

CGPA accumulates. An early strong semester gives you breathing room in final year.
